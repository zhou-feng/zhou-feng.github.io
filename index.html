Feng Zhou (周峰)

I'm a PhD candidate at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, where I work with Prof. Benjamin Becker in the neuSCAN Lab.

I'm currently visiting the Cognitive and Affective Neuroscience Lab, directed by Prof. Tor Wager.

My research uses neuroimaging and physiological methods to understand the neurophysiology of affective processes (e.g., emotion and empathy) and how they are shaped by cognitive influences.

The longer-term goal of his research is to develop novel treatment approaches for emotional dysregulation disorders

For more information, please see my publications and curriculum vitae (CV).
